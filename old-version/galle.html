Explore Galle Fort

The Timeless Fort

The southern jewel of Sri Lanka, Galle is a living testament to European colonial influence in South Asia. The UNESCO-listed Galle Fort is a network of cobblestone streets, boutique shops, art galleries, and historic Dutch villas, all encircled by monumental ramparts that have stood for centuries against the ocean's waves.

Walking along the fort walls at sunset, with the iconic lighthouse in the distance, is an experience like no other. Beyond the fort, Galle's vibrant culture and pristine golden beaches offer the perfect balance of history and relaxation.

Must-Visit Highlights

  • Galle Fort
  • Dutch Hospital
  • Maritime Museum
  • Dutch Reformed Church
  • Unawatuna Beach
  • Galle Lighthouse
